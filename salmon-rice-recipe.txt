Ingredients:
- Brown Rice
- Black Beans
- Canned Corn
- Red Onion
- Tomato
- Olive Oil
- Kale
- Rainbow Carrots
- Pickled Bell Peppers
- Cajun Style Salmon

Directions:
1. Cook rice in rice cooker
2. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
3. Dice carrots, kale, red onion, tomato
4. Sautee kale and carrots in wok until tender, then leave on low heat
5. Cook salmon for 15min
6. Mix cooked rice, black beans, corn, raw red onion, and tomato with a splash of olive oil
7. Take kale and carrot sautee off and place over rice mezlca
8. Serve with salmon and enjoy
